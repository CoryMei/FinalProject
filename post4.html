Although problems brought by social media continue, But things seem to be moving in the right direction, with more and more tech companies being sued over privacy and data issues, and more and more people talking about fear and confusion over social media. But before the problem is completely solved, there are a few things we can do as an individual:

1. Silencing notifications and deleting wasteful apps
2. Don't passively accept the content recommended to you, but actively acquire it
3. Verify the authenticity of any information before sharing it
4. The world is very beautiful, go offline to have a look and connect with it
5. setting screen time limits
